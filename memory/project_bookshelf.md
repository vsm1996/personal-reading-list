Building "Bookshelf", a Frontend Mentor Product Challenge: a personal reading tracker with book search (Open Library/Google Books API), shelf management, reading progress, annual goals, year-in-review statistics, and a guest mode with 45 pre-loaded books.

**Why:** Portfolio piece demonstrating full-stack skills, product thinking, AI collaboration, and design taste.

**How to apply:** Full spec lives in spec/, guidance in guidance/, starter tokens in starter/. Brand kit is the design source of truth (guidance/brand-kit.md). Design-it-yourself features (year-in-review, book discovery, reading progress UX) require asking clarifying questions before implementing.
